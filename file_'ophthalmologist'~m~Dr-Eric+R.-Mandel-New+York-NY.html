Biography

Mandel Vision is New York's premiere Laser Vision Correction Center specializing in LASIK and PRK. Our Medical Director and corneal specialist, Eric R. Mandel Vision utilizes the latest technology and techniques in laser vision correction today. Call today to schedule your FREE laser vision correction evaluation. And, don't forget to go to our website, www.mandelvision.com, so you can discover the latest advances in LASIK and PRK today. We are New York's Premiere LASIK Center.
