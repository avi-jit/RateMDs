My procedure included breast implants, a lift on one breast, as well as, resizing of the areola on one breast (as I had asymmetrical breasts). I am now 4 months post-op and I have gotten a number of compliments on how natural they look. I work in a professional environment, so for me, that was very important. I had done alot of research before the surgery and thought I knew exactly what I wanted. I absolutely love my results and I am glad I trusted Dr Jugenberg's judgement. I highly recommend visiting Dr Jugenberg even if this is a decision you are still contemplating. I wish I visited him sooner.
