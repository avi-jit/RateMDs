Biography

Dr. Farrah Sunderji graduated from the New England College of Optometry and further completed a residency in Pediatrics and Vision Therapy. She is passionate about eyecare and believes in preventative health for all ages. She frequently speaks on Global and City Breakfast TV and writes for the Calgary's Child about various eye health topics. She runs a dry eye clinic and enjoys fitting contact lenses. Her practice Eyedeology is part of Centennial Clinic - an integrated approach to healthcare.
