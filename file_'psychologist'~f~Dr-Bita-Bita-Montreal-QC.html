Biography

Dr. Bita is a psychologist working from Montreal, West Island, and Laval. She is the clinical director and founder of Clinic Dr. Bita Inc. training and supervising psychologists and psychotherapists in Montreal and Ontario. She is also the author of 3 published books. Although graduated with her PhD in 2009, Dr. Bita never ceased acquiring new specialities. She specializes in the treatment of various psychological disorders such as sex and couples therapy, PTSD, anger and anxiety management, compulsive behaviour, and pain management. Her approach is eclectic with an emphasis on cognitive behavioural therapy and sensory motor psychotherapy. With an empathic and warm nature she believes in empowering her clients into reaching their potentials and becoming their own therapists and managers.
