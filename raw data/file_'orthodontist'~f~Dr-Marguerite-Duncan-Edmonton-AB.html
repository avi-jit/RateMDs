Biography

My name is Dr. Marguerite Duncan. I am an Orthodontist (dental specialist). I have been practicing in Edmonton for eleven years. In 2004 I moved to Edmonton from Nova Scotia to attend post graduate training in general practice at the University of Alberta Hospital. After a few years in family dental practice, I returned to to the University of Alberta for another 3 years of training to become a specialist in Orthodontics. I have been a licensed Orthodontic Specialist in Alberta since 2010. I love smiles, especially warm and confident confident ones. I have dedicated my career to transforming smiles, encouraging self-confidence, and enhancing oral health. I am a wife and a mother, in addition to a professional with a career to tend. I know the challenges that adults face in coordinating and providing for all of the needs of their families as well as themselves. Balancing work and responsibilities, while making time for rest and rejuvenation is merely a dream for most of us. Where I wish to help is by providing service to you in the most efficient and reliable manner possible, ensuring you are fully equipped with the knowledge and tools to make informed choices appropriate for you. Thank you for taking a moment to get to know me. I very much look forward to getting to know you!
