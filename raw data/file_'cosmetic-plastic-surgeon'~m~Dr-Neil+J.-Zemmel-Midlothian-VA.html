Biography

Board-certified plastic and reconstructive surgeon Dr. Neil J. Zemmel offers state-of-the-art care at his private practice Richmond Aesthetic Surgery in Richmond, Virginia. Having undergone extensive training in a variety of innovative surgical and non-surgical procedures, Dr. Zemmel provides his patients with the safest, most effective techniques to help them reach their ideal outcomes. From facial procedures to body and breast surgeries, the Virginia surgeon is committed to working with patients to help them address their physical concerns to ultimately makeover their confidence and feel beautiful.
