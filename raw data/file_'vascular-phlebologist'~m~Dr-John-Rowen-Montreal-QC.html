Biography

After graduating from Queen’s University Medical School, Dr. Rowen completed his residency in Emergency Medicine at McGill, then served in St-Mary’s emergency department, where he has now been for over 25 years. He started his training for phlebology (varicose veins) in 2000 in the USA, and has been treating patients with injectable botox and fillers since 2004. He is a member of the American College of Phlebology, the Canadian Society of Phlebology and Canadian Association of Aesthetic Medicine (CAAM). In the year 2007 Dr. Rowen worked with and learned from some of the best aesthetic doctors in Canada. This experience has influenced Dr Rowen immensely in his pursuit of excellence for every patient he treats. He truly enjoys esthetic treatments (such as neuromodulator and hyaluronic acid injections), and strives to make sure all of his patients look and feel natural and beautiful.
