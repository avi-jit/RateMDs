Biography

Dr. Kathleen Bente received her Doctor of Chiropractic degree in 1992 from New York Chiropractic College in Seneca Falls, New York. She opened her Chiropractic office in 1993 in Westwood, New Jersey and has been practicing in this location ever since. This year she is celebrating her 25th Anniversary. Dr. Bente continues to advance her knowledge and education with post-graduate studies and most recently received her Certification in Clinicial Nutrition. She currently offers a Comprehensive Nutritional Program to her patients who desire to improve their health. In addition, Dr. Bente graduated from the Swedish Institute of Massage in New York in 1986 and received her degree as a Licensed Massage Therapist. Dr. Bente utilizes various technologies and services to speed healing time and recovery of her patients' conditions including Avant Laser Therapy, Multi-Radiance Laser, Rapid Release Technology (RRT), Celluma Light Therapy, Kinesiology Tape, Recovery RX devices, Whole Body Vibration, and Meridian Tapping Therapy
