Biography

Welcome to my Practice Website. I am internationally Board Certified in Plastic Surgery by both the Royal College of Surgeons of Canada, and the British Association of Plastic, Reconstructive and Aesthetic Surgeons. I am also a Fellowship-trained specialist in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery for women. Based in Vancouver, my practice philosophy focuses on providing natural, exceptional surgical results, with individualized and compassionate care. My practice is centered on providing beautiful, natural and long-lasting results for breast cancer survivors using microsurgery or implants, and I use the same approach for my cosmetic practice, specializing in rejuvenation surgery of the breasts and abdomen.
