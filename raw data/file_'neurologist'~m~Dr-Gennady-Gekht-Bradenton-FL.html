Biography

Pain Management Specialist, Dr. Gekht, specializes in neuromuscular disorders related to painful neuropathies of the legs, hands, neck and back. Dr. Gekht also performs the full realm of interventional pain management and is an expert in treating vertebral compression fractures and pelvic fractures. Dr. Gekht completed two fellowships: one in Pain Management from Mayo Clinic and another in Electrophysiology and Neuromuscular Disorders from Emory University in Atlanta. Dr. Gekht served as Chief Resident at Emory University, where he also received his doctor of medicine.
