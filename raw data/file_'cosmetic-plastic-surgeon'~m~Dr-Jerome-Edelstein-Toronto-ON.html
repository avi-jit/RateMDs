I'm currently 2 months post op and I had the most amazing experience with Dr. Edelstein and his office. He performed my breast augmentation in April 2017 and came off the recommendation from my mom. He is very active on Real Self and recently won the RealSelf Top 100 Award. From beginning to end he was so knowledgeable and respectful. He really took the time to understand what my goal was and recommended sizes from there. His staff is also amazing, his nurse Heather does your sizing appointment and helps you choose your implant size. Her knowledge and experience is indispensable. She was such a great part of the process, using her experience with past procedures in helping you make the right choice. For instance, I had a back and forth battle with myself when trying to finalize a size, I couldn't decide between 370CC and 415CC. Heather told me that just about everyone in my situation who picks the smaller size comes to their post-op follow up wishing they would have chosen bigger. Also once the implant is put under your muscle you do lose a little so they tend to be a bit smaller than just trying them on. I'm so glad she was there to guide me in my decision as I went for the bigger size and I couldn't be happier ---- I know now that I would have been unhappy with the smaller. Another point I wanted to touch on was Dr. Edelstein's bedside manner--he is so kind and comforting. He checked on me before and after my surgery when in hospital and his attitude made my time there a breeze knowing I was in good hands. He is also readily available to answer any questions / concerns post op. A day or two after my surgery I started to react to the pain medication, my entire body was very itchy. I called his after-hours line as this was a Saturday afternoon, spoke with his nurse and within 15 minutes Dr. Edelstein called me directly. He let me know it was a normal reaction and that I can take Benadryl to ease the itching. When I went for my 2 week follow up in the hospital, the nurse there who helped with my bandages was even raving about Dr. Edelstein, saying he was the best around and that she sees women fly from all over Canada to get their procedures performed by him. I would absolutely recommend Dr. Edelstein for anyone searching for the best cosmetic surgeon.

Dr. Edelstein was a recommendation from a friend who had seen him for a breast augmentation in early 2017. She couldn't stop raving about how great he was and her results were fantastic, so of course, I had to get myself in for an appointment. My consult was late September 2017, and my surgery was booked very shortly thereafter for mid-October (the consult appointment takes the longest wait, but surgery dates are quick!). First, the consult: I am a very petite and slender individual who was naturally blessed/cursed with very little breast tissue (5'6", 115lb, small bone structure). My goal was to have two small handfuls at the end. I wanted something natural, and something that would maintain my slender profile. I was clear about not wanting to be top heavy. Sure enough, Dr. Edelstein listened and delivered. He gave me three size recommendations of which I went with the smallest: 280cc low profile plus cohesive gel, a slightly wider, flatter implant known for delivering a more natural slope and overall profile. The PERFECT choice for me. Dr. Edelstein's greatest attribute is perhaps his easy going, personable demeanour. He exudes a quiet confidence, and that in turn, puts you as a patient completely at ease. I opted for the surgery date at Humber River Hospital vs. the private surgery clinic, and have to say the entire process was smooth and enjoyable. Overall, I was impressed by how the hospital operated - from the text message notifications, to the bedside care. All fantastic. In terms of recovery, as promised, it was a very smooth process which is undoubtedly attributed to Dr. Edelstein's elevated surgical technique/approach. I did end up with every possible post-op side effect (from the hypersensitivity, to the mondor's cords, to the crackling under the skin) but hey, nothing I didn't prepare myself to undergo and all considered normal to expect. I am now almost 5 months post-op and my breasts are healing great. So pleased! Best money I've spent and exactly the outcome I had hoped for.
