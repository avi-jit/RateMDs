Biography

Dr. Lisa E. Heuer's clinical expertise in the neurobiology of weight management, nutrition, and exercise physiology has made her an expert in the fight against obesity. She returns to Rockland County after training and working in New York City for over a decade.Dr. Heuer graduated from the Mount Sinai School of Medicine in New York City and holds a Bachelor of Science with Honors from Cornell University. Before entering medical school, Dr. Heuer did graduate work in Clinical Nutrition at New York University Dr. Heuer's program offers a comprehensive, medical approach. There is a science to weight loss based on Evidence Based Medicine. The only way to maximize fat loss and keep the weight off permanently is to change the hormonal environment in your body, in essence, to change your neurophysiology. This approach is what differentiates Dr. Heuer's practice from other weight loss options. And you'll find comfort in her philosophy of: "I don't just treat you, I'm with you in your fight for better health through weight loss and management until you succeed!"
