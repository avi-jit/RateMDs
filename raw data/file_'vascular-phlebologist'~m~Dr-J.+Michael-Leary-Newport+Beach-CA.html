He convinced me of this and said that my leg would be better and healthier than before. He said the other veins would take over and they were doing that now anyway. The procedure itself is very unpleasant with many injections and compression stocking that is hot and cut deeply into the back of leg and left ligature marks for many months after. Biography

If you have venous disease, whether it be varicose veins, spider veins or any other vascular condition, you will be in the best of hands with Dr. Leary, a Board Certified Phlebologist. Through his ability to expertly diagnose and skillfully perform the correct treatment to heal the effects of venous disease, you will receive state-of-the art treatment with minimally invasive medical technology and excellent follow up care. As a Phlebologist, he will coordinate every step of your treatment and follow up care. You are invited to come in for a private consultation and screening. During the consultation you will receive a comprehensive examination where he will answer all of your questions and explain how the venous system works and why you may be experiencing symptoms. The consultation also includes an Ultrasound of your legs. The Ultrasound assists in selecting the most effective vascular treatment for your specific needs and best desired result. For your convenience, all procedures are performed at California Vein Specialists, from the initial consultation through post-procedure care.
