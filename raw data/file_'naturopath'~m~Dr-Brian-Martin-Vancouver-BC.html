Biography

Dr. Brian Martin BSc ND, is a naturopathic doctor and diplomat in Anti-aging from A4M with over 22 years of clinical practice specializing in optimal aging medicine, chronic conditions, infertility and weight loss. At EnerChanges Clinic optimal aging medicine focuses on how to enhance a person’s overall quality of life, this includes the proper testing and safe use of bio-hormones, including Human Growth Hormone (HGH) decreasing stubborn weight (using the Rapid Metabolic HCG program), treating traumatic brain injury (TBI) and improving overall quality of life, energy, mood, sexual function and sleep. Dr. Brian Martin and EnerChanges Clinic are recommended by Suzanne Somers in her bestselling book, “Breakthrough”.
