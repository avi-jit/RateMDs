Biography

Dr. Mohammad Bahasadri is a board-certified general surgeon who believes in honesty, integrity, and respect for patients as the cornerstones of his practice. He trained in general surgery at the University of Saskatchewan followed by a fellowship in advanced laparoscopic surgery at McMaster University in Hamilton. Dr. Bahasadri specializes in advanced laparoscopic surgery of gastrointestinal tract cancer, abdominal wall hernias, gallbladder disease and diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy of upper and lower G.I tract (Gastroscopy and Colonoscopy). As a member of the most prestigious societies and surgical organizations in North America and the world Dr. Bahasadri applies the most advanced surgical methods and techniques to provide his patients with the highest level of quality care, thereby minimizing discomfort and shortening recovery time. His practice is at Etobicoke General Hospital, William Osler Health System. 89 Humber College Blvd, Suite 203, Toronto ON M9V 4B8. Tel:(416)913-6457, Fax:(416)913-6527
