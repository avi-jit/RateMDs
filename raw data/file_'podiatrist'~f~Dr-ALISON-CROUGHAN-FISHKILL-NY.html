Biography

Dr. Alison Croughan recently joined our Long Island Offices and she has been a great compliment to our practice. She is a young, energetic and knowledgeable podiatrist who is great with children and geriatrics! Her mission is to "always be a competent physician who will diagnose and formulate both conservative and surgical treatment options for common foot and ankle disorders as well as congenital conditions, diabetic foot infections, and management of foot and ankle trauma. My focus is directed towards developing and maintaining positive patient interaction and to always translate medical terminology into layman's terms to assist patients in their health care decision making. My mission is to provide comprehensive care so that my patients can walk confidently knowing that great foot care is indicative of their overall health."
