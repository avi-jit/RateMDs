Biography

Dr. Janna Bentley was born and raised in Kelowna, BC â a rarity here among residents in the Beautiful Okanagan! She received her Bachelor of Science in Honours Physiology from the University of British Columbia in 1997, and then received her Medical Doctorate from the University of Alberta in 2003. From 2003-2005 Dr. Bentley completed her residency training at the University of Alberta in family medicine with a focus on phlebology (the study of venous disease) and a special interest in Dermatology-related issues. All of her elective time was spent doing rotations in phlebology and Dermatology-related areas in Calgary, Edmonton, Toronto, Montreal, and Vancouver. After completing her training Dr. Bentley returned to Kelowna for good... and where she and her husband love living and raising a family of three lovely children. Dr. Bentley joined the Kelowna Vein and Laser Centre in June 2005 and began her career in phlebology. In the fall of 2005 she became a partner at Okanagan Vein & Skin Care Centre, as well as the medical staff at Kelowna General Hospital. FOUNDING PARTNER & VISIONARY Dr. Bentley was one of the original founding partners of Lakeshore Vein & Aesthetics Clinic in December 2008, and now is the owner and Medical Director. In 2009, Dr. Bentley became a board certified diplomate of the American College of Phlebology (ACPh) and in 2011 she was elected as a Fellow of the Canadian Society of Phlebology. Dr. Bentley is qualified as a medical sonographer, specializing in varicose veins as a Registered Vascular Technologist (RVT). In early 2012, Dr Bentley were honoured to receive Clinical Faculty Appointments with the UBC Faculty of Medicine. Dr Bentley, and Lakeshore Vein & Aesthetics Clinic, continue to support and help the new UBC Okanagan medical school (officially called the Southern Medical Program) by being one of Kelowna's â and the BC Interior's â UBCO Dermatology Teaching Centers. Working full-time, with thousands of referrals per year from other GPs and specialists, Dr. Bentley has become one of the most experienced physicians in cosmetic medicine in the BC Interior (Okanagan/Thompson) region. Dr. Bentley is a regular speaker at phlebology conferences (disease and health of veins) and is invited by Allergan to exclusive panels/conferences for Canada's top ~75 cosmetic clinics. CURRENTLY Dr. Janna Bentley is a member in good standing with: - Canadian Society of Phlebology (Fellow, VP of Education, Executive Board) - American College of Phlebology (Diplomate) - Canadian Medical Association (CMA) - College of Physicians & Surgeons of BC (CPSBC) - British Columbia Medical Association (BCMA) - College of Family Physicians of Canada (CFPC) - American Board of Laser Surgery - American Registry for Diagnostic Medical SonographyÂ® (ARDMSÂ®) The visionary behind the creation of the Lakeshore Vein & Aesthetics Clinic, Dr. Bentley specializes in Phlebology (varicose vein disease), from sclerotherapy to endovenous laser treatment (EVLT), to pulsed dye laser treatment of spider veins.
