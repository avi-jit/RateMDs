Biography

Sigfredo Aldarondo, MD, FCCP has practiced Pulmonary and Critical Care Medicine in Central Florida since 1986 and Sleep Medicine since 2004. A graduate of the University of Puerto Rico School of Medicine, he has consistently been recognized as one of the top physicians in his field and has served in different capacities with the University of Central Florida, Florida Hospital leadership, as well as diverse community and faith-based initiatives in the Central Florida area. Dr. Aldarondo has a private practice in Winter Park where he, his colleague Manuel Betancourt, MD and his staff, strive to treat, educate and partner with their patients on lung health.
