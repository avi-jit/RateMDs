I have been a patient of Dr. Kakani’s for several years now as I had been suffering from terrible allergies and chronic sinus infections. Dr. Kakani has helped me not only get my allergies under control but explained every option I had to improve and prevent further infections (the menu as he refers to it). After exhausting all of my options, a scan of my sinuses, and a very thorough review of those scans, we decided to take the surgical route (without any pressure - he seriously only wants you to do what you feel comfortable with). I was extremely extremely nervous as I had never received anesthesia or had any other prior surgeries before. I am in my third week of recovery and feeling wonderful thus far. Dr. Kakani successfully fixed my deviated septum, opened my sinus cavities, and cleared out my damaged mucosal lining. His infectious smile and intelligence gave me the comfort that I needed to go through with the procedure and thus far I am extremely happy I did. He is a wonderful doctor and I highly recommend him if you are in need of an ENT!
