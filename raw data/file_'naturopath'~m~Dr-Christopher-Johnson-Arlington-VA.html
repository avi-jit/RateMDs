Biography

Dr. Johnson was born and raised in Alexandria, VA. He received a BA in Religious Studies from the University of VIrginia. He earned his Doctor of Naturopathic Medicine at Southwest College of Naturopathic Medicine in Tempe, AZ. He has been in private practice since 2008, specializing in the use of homeopathy and nutrition to treat various conditions. He currently serves as Editor-in-Chief of the American Journal of Homeopathic Medicine (which has been in continuous publication since 1909).
