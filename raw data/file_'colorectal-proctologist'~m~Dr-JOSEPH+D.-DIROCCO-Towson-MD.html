Most of the people who call these offices are already in physical pain or distress of some kind, as I am, and to be treated this horrendously before even getting to see the doctor is shocking. It astounds me that someone like Dr. DiRocco, who has so many overwhelmingly positive reviews on these websites, could have office staff this rude and aggressive. But if these are the people that he hires as his office staff and assistants, then I certainly have no faith in his judgment as a person or a doctor. I will gladly go much farther out of my way to seek treatment from someone who treats patients (and prospective patients) with compassion and respect.

Biography

Dr. DiRocco is a surgeon with Greater Baltimore Colorectal Specialists. A graduate of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine in Bronx, New York, Dr. DiRocco completed his surgical residency and a surgical resident research fellowship at State University of New York Upstate Medical University. He went on to complete a colon and rectal surgery fellowship at New York-Presbyterian/Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. Board-certified in Surgery and Colon and Rectal Surgery, Dr. DiRocco is interested in minimally invasive techniques and is trained in sphincter-sparing surgery for rectal cancer. Joseph DiRocco, MD, MBA, FACS, has been named Director of Gastrointestinal Oncology for the Sandra & Malcolm Berman Cancer Institute at GBMC.
