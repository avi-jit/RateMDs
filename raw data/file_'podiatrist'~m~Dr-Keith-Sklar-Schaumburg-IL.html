Biography

What makes me Unique? I don't compromise beauty to correct the function of your feet. My surgical procedures allow for less pain, a quicker recovery and results that you want to see. I also apply what I learn. Having been in practice for 30 years, I have treated over 60,000 patients and performed more than 9,000 surgical procedures. With all this experience, my procedures are continually modified; thus evolving into sound, minimally invasive surgical procedures. By studying and working hard, I became board certified by two distinct boards. I continue to maintain these certifications through retesting. I am board certified by the American Board of Foot and Ankle Surgery and by the American Board of Podiatric Medicine. I am also a Fellow of The American College of Foot & Ankle Orthopedics & Medicine and a Fellow of The American College of Foot & Ankle Surgeons. When not treating patients, I have a great wife who I love spending time with. Married for 30 years, we have three wonderful daughters. One daughter is a Podiatrist, one daughter is a nurse, and my other daughter is currently in nursing school. I also like working out to P90x, playing basketball, running and rock climbing. Did I mention I have a cool dog - a Tibetan Terrier - named Reggie.
