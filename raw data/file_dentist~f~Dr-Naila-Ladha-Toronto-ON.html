Biography

Dr. Ladha obtained an Honours Bachelor of Science degree with High Distinction from the University of Toronto in 2004. She graduated with a DMD degree from Boston University ‘s Goldman School of Dental Medicine in 2008. Dr. Ladha is committed to quality patient care with the primary goal of achieving optimal oral health and beauty for all of her patients. She is happy to be working with Dawson Dental Centres where these principles are shared. Along with general and cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Ladha offers Invisalign Invisible braces. To connect with Dr. Ladha, you can follow her on Twitter @SmilesbyNaila or on Linked In.
