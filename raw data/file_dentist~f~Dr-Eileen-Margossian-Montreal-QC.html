Biography

Dr. Margossian is passionate about dentistry and is dedicated to ensuring the health of her patient's smiles. She possesses a degree in Microbiology from McGill University and a degree in Dentistry from Université de Montréal. She completed a multidisciplinary residency at the Montreal General Hospital (affiliated to McGill University). Subsequently, she worked as a clinician within the dental surgery clinic at the Université de Montréal for 13 years and has had her own private practice since 1989. She received special recognition from the Department of Stomatology/Surgery at Université de Montréal in appreciation of her outstanding work as a clinician. Dr. Margossian has regularly pursued continuing education courses to keep up with the latest advances in dentistry, especially in the field of cosmetics and implantology. Dr. Margossian has as much fun helping her patient today as she did when she first started practicing all those years ago.
