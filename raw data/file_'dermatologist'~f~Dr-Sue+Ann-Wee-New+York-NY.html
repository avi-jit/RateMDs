Biography

Dr. Wee is a fellowship-trained and board-certified laser, cosmetic, and general dermatologist. She earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Stanford University and a Doctorate of Medicine degree from Stanford Medical School where she graduated at the top of her class. She completed her dermatology residency at New York University in the Ronald O. Perelman Department of Dermatology followed by a Laser Surgery and Cosmetic Fellowship program at the renowned SkinCare Physicians with Harvard and Yale faculty. Dr. Wee has lectured extensively and written numerous peer-reviewed scientific articles in dermatology. Her advanced training enables her to blend artistry and an eye for detail with innovative and scientific approaches for a wide range of skin concerns in patients of all skin types. Dr. Wee believes in a compassionate and strong partnership with her patients and tailors complete skin care to the individual.
