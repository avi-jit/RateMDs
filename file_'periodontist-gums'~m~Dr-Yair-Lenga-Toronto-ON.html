Biography

As a University of Toronto alumni majoring in General Dental Degree with specialty training in Periodontics, it's safe to say that Dr. Yair Lenga always knew where he was headed. After completing a prestigious masters degree in Tissue-Healing Philosophy. Dr. Lenga focused on preventative care and education as being the keys to optimal dental health. On a Daily basis Dr. Lenga manages to restore patients back to their natural state of health with periodontal and reconstructive procedures, but his true passion is exceeding the expectations of the patient by making sure they leave St. Clair Perio clinic with a smile. With his consistent determination to go above and beyond, it is no surprise that Dr. Lenga has become recognized within his industry. Managing to align himself with the Royal College of Dental Surgeons of Canada and the American Academy of Periodontology. Dr. Lenga's efforts within the realm of periodontics have given him the honorary title as a Diplomat of the American Board of Periodontology. In his spare time, his adventurous spirit and love for an adrenaline rush takes him from snowboarding slopes to hiking trails and far flug destinations. As a strong believer in leading by example, Dr. Lenga maintains a healthy lifestyle by learning new cooking techniques so, don't be afraid to share the recipe of your favorite healthy dish on your next visit to the St. Clair Perio clinic!
