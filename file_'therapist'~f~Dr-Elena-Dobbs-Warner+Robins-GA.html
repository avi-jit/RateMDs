Biography

Talented therapist with an active LPC and LMHC license, as well as specialization in hypnotherapy and certification as an RRT hypnotherapist, which is an organization that helps victims of traumatic experiences leave the past behind through a very specialized and very targeted and effective hypnotherapy. Dr. Dobbs specializes also in Mindfulness Techniques and teaches them to peers in different sites around the country. In addition, she is certified as a Neurofeedback Practitioner and a BAUD practitioner, both systems dealing with training the brain to eliminate cravings, focus better and lessen anxiety symptoms at the very source. Dr. Dobbs has an MS in Clinical Psychology, a PhD in Counseling Psychology and is finishing another Doctorate Degree in Clinical Psychology which will allow her to become a Licensed Psychologist in the near future
