Biography

Dr. Callum Cowan BSc (Hons), ND is a leading authority and practitioner of high performance medicine who is most recognized for his work with professional athletes both nationally and internationally. His approach focuses on maximizing the health of his patients to get them feeling amazing, performing phenomenally and maintaining optimal health to prevent the occurrence of illness/injury. Dr. Cowan integrates the latest and most advanced modern scientific diagnostics with a comprehensive medical understanding of the body and how it works to identify the root causes of occurring issues and key areas for improvement. His treatments are tailored to meet the specific and unique needs of each patient for the most effective results and long-term health benefits. Dr. Cowan is trained and able to treat a wide variety of medical conditions and health concerns, however, the majority of his work is centered on issues relating to what he calls “The Super 7” - Energy, Weight, Digestion, Mood, Immune, Hormone, Athletic Performance
