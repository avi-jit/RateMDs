Biography

Dr Cangas is from Dallas City, IL. He married his wife Zoe (rhymes with Joe) in February, 2014. Zoe obtained her degree in Kineseology from Eastern Illinois University. They have a daughter, Alexis (12) and baby Ellyn, born February 2015. After high school, Joe served in the. Army.. He then attended Southeastern Community College, Western Illinois University, and the University of Illinois College of Medicine. His pediatric residency was completed at the University of Missouri in 2004.. He enjoys watching college sports, is a Cardinals fan, is an active member of the Columbia and Crestwood/Sunset Hills Kiwanis, and a board member of Life Network of Southern Illinois. After working in Salem, IL for two years, he wanted to be closer to 'the city'. So, in 2006, he started Illini Pediatrics, after seeing the need for a pediatrician in Monroe County. Together with Illini Pediatrics and Helmets First, he hopes to keep the children of the area safe and healthy.
