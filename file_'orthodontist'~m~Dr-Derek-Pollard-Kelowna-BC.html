Biography

Dr. Derek Pollard was raised in Coquitlam, BC. He began his post-secondary education close to his home at Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, BC, where he received a Bachelor of Science with honors, majoring in Kinesiology and minoring in Biochemistry. Following Graduation from SFU, he moved out to Winnipeg, Manitoba after being accepted in the University of Manitobaâs Faculty of Dentistry. Four years later, Dr. Pollard graduated with honors with a Doctor of Dental Medicine. Over the next year, Dr. Pollard first practiced dentistry at two separate general practices in Winnipeg. He also worked for Health Canada, travelling up to remote First Nationâs communities in Northern Manitoba to provide dental care to these under-serviced areas. He also held a part-time faculty member position at the University of Manitoba in the Undergraduate Orthodontics department. In 2008, Dr. Pollard decided to pursue his passion in Orthodontics and head back to the University of Manitoba after being accepted into the Graduate Orthodontics program. After 3 years of study, he graduated with a Masters in Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics. in 2011, Dr. Pollard and his wife Courtney moved out to the beautiful Okanagan. The Kelowna area is a great fit for them since they love to keep active, spending time golfing, snowboarding, biking, and wakeboarding. They also added two beautiful kids into the mix. Dr. Pollard opened the doors to his Kelowna office, Mission Creek Orthodontics, in 2011 and followed this up with his satellite office, Pollard & Perrin Orthodontics, in 2014. Dr. Derek Pollard is a member of the Canadian Association of Orthodontics, Canadian Dental Association, College of Dental Surgeons of British Columbia, and is a fellow of the Royal College of Dentists in Canada. He is also the current President of the Kelowna & District Dental Society (KDDS) and President and Founder of the Kelowna Orthodontic Study Club (KOSC). Dr. Pollard looks forward to discussing your orthodontic needs and delivering on his promise of a beautiful, functional, and healthy smile.
