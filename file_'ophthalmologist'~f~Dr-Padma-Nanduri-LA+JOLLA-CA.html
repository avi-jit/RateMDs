Biography

Padma Nanduri, M.D., F.A.C.S. is the lead surgeon and physician at Dr. Nanduri’s Eye Surgeon’s Medical Group based in La Jolla,CA, County of San Diego CA. Dr. Nanduri received her medical degree from the prestigious University of Rochester, School of Medicine and Dentistry, Rochester New York and is a Board Certified Ophthalmologist. Dr. Nanduri previously served as assistant clinical professor, at UCSD School of Medicine training ophthalmology residents and fellows. At Dr. Nanduri’s Associated Eye Surgeon’s Medical Group, we are fully dedicated to improving and preserving sight, offering comprehensive vision care for any disease or disorder of the eye. The physicians at Dr. Nanduri’s Eye Surgeon’s Medical Group put special emphasis on bringing our patients the most advanced technology in ophthalmology available in a private practice. The experienced and skilled physician’s staff includes Libro E. DiZinno, M.D. Eye Physician and Surgeon, Glaucoma consultation and surgery, and Martin Friedlander, M.D. Ph.D , retinal specialist. About Our Practice: Dr. Nanduri’s Associated Eye Surgeon’s Medical Group is a full comprehensive ophthalmology practice offering eye care ranging from routine eye exams to complex surgical procedures of the eye. The physicians at Dr. Nanduri’s Associated Eye Surgeon’s Medical Group have performed thousands of delicate eye surgeries such as cataract surgery with advanced lens implant techniques, LASIK, and other refractive surgery procedures, glaucoma surgery, pterygium removal with and without AmnioGraft, retinal surgery, eye lid and strabismus surgery and have extensive experience in diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the eye, eye trauma and disorders. Dr. Nanduri’s Associated Eye Surgeon’s Medical Group is available for ocular emergencies 24 hours a day. Contact via the office telephone number 858-450-1010
