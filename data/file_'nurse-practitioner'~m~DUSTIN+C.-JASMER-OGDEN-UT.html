Biography

I see children (4+) for Medication Management for ADHD, Anger/Aggression, Anxiety, Autism, Depression, Bipolar, Aggression, behavioral issues and a variety of other issues as well. I also see Adults for a variety of conditions including Anxiety, Depression, ADHD, Bipolar, PTSD, Hormone Replacement, Low Testosterone, Menopause, PCOS and Weight Loss with a family practice perspective. I'm a Family Nurse Practitioner that has received extra training in behavioral health. My focus is in behavioral health medication management. I use a comprehensive approach to caring for patients and realize that medication is not the only answer for these issues. I think it is important for patients to have choices and be partners in their care.I want my patients to feel like they have a caring partner and someone who will listen to their concerns because ultimately it is their life, their body, their mind that is being treated.
