I was so impressed when I went to see Dr. Massoumi for the first time. The office staff were welcoming and friendly and answered any questions that I had. She recommended nature throid to help with this and an adaptogen called Adapt 232. I ordered the Adapt 232 off a website she provided me with and received it a few days later. I took it and noticed an increase in energy a few days later.
