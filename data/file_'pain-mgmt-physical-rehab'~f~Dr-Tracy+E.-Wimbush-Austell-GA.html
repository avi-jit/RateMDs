Biography

Tracy E. Wimbush, M.D. is board certified by the American Board of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, with a subspecialty in Pain Medicine, and by the American Board of Pain Medicine. She also holds a board certification by the American Board of Emergency Medicine. Dr. Wimbush provides pain management and nonsurgical orthopaedic care to patients, utilizing her training in nonsurgical pain management of musculoskeletal, neuromuscular, sports injuries and work injuries. Dr. Wimbush’s practice includes orthopaedic medicine, interventional spinal injections, electro diagnostic medicine, emergency medicine and physical medicine. She has special interest in treating difficult conditions such as fibromyalgia, post-herpetic neuralgia, and migraines. In addition to standard interventions for pain management, Dr. Wimbush also utilizes the assistance of ultrasound guidance for both diagnosis and therapeutic interventions.
