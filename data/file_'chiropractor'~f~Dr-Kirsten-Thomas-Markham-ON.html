Biography

Since opening in balance chiropractic + acupuncture clinic in January 2009, Dr. Thomas is very proud that she has been voted Best Chiropractor in Markham in the Reader's Choice Awards - 5 years in a row for 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013 and 2014! In 2014, she was also voted Top Chiropractor in the Top Choice Awards. Dr. Thomas greatly appreciates the support she has received from her patients. Dr. Thomas strives to help her patients by completing a very thorough initial assessment, by offering a variety of chiropractic and soft tissue techniques and by providing a personalized exercise rehabilitation program and lifestyle suggestions. She enjoys sharing the benefits of over ten years of ongoing continuing education with her patients. Dr. Thomas first attended the University of Western Ontario graduating in 2000 with an Honours Bachelor of Science degree in Kinesiology. From there she attended the world renowned Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College in Toronto, Ontario graduating in 2004. Dr. Thomas completed the medical acupuncture program and further advanced courses in functional assessment, advanced needling techniques, acupuncture for headaches, sports injuries, stress related problems and women's health issues at McMaster University. As well, she completed the one year fellowship program and Webster Technique certification from the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association which focuses on chiropractic care for pregnant women and children. She has taken additional courses on pre-natal chiropractic and pediatrics from many notable lecturers. In addition, Dr. Thomas is an Active Release Techniques (ART®) provider certified in Spine, Upper Extremity, Lower Extremity, Nerve Entrapment and Active Palpation protocols. She is also a Certified Golf Fitness Instructor with Titleist Performance Institute to help golfers improve their game by improving their fitness levels. She has attended numerous workshops and seminars on exercise rehabilitation and strongly believes in its importance in helping her patients reach their goals. Dr. Thomas has certifications with Thompson Technique and the Motion Palpation Institute in Spine and Extremity adjusting procedures. Dr. Thomas has completed Level 1 and 2 Cranial Adjusting techniques which is very helpful in the treatment of headaches and concussions. She has completed advanced custom orthotic therapy and condition based orthotic therapy courses with The Orthotic Group. She also is certified as a Personal Trainer Specialist with Can-Fit Pro and as a Level 2 Ski Instructor with the Canadian Ski Instructors Alliance. In 2010, Dr. Thomas completed the animal chiropractic certification program through the Healing Oasis Wellness Centre of Canada/Veterinary Chiropractic Learning Centre in Brantford. This course consisted of 210 hours of supervised instruction in the treatment of canines, felines and equines. She then completed the Advanced Canine Techniques course in 2013. Dr. Thomas is the first certified animal chiropractor practicing in Markham and is a certified member of the College of Animal Chiropractors. Dr. Thomas's passions are fitness, golf, skiing, curling, ballet, reading and her cat Starr. Dr. Thomas and her husband Cameron, welcomed their first baby, Curtis in August 2014.
