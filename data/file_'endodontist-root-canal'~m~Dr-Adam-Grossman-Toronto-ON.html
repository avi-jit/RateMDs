As far as I'm concerned, Dr. Grossman is the best endodontist in Toronto. I was terrified to get my first root canal last week. It was for a lower back tooth that was getting infected. Adding to the stress was the fact I couldn't open my mouth very wide due to TMJ and an arthritic jaw. I had heard so many horror stories about root canals so I was literally shaking when I got into Dr. Grossman's office. Was I surprised at what ensued. First, of all, I barely felt the two needles he gave me for freezing ... and I felt absolutely no pain during the one-hour root canal procedure. When the root canal was done, his staff said I would need to take heavy duty painkillers when the freezing wore off. Well, I went home and waited and waited all day and I waited the next day too. No pain. No sore jaw. Absolutely no discomfort at all. In fact, it was honestly less discomfort than getting a filling. Dr. Grossman is very talented root canal dentist ... a total pro. I know I was quite a challenge for him because of my inability to open my mouth very wide ... and I should add that I am very petite (only 4-foot-8) so my mouth is very small to begin with. That Dr. Grossman was able to work around this and perform a painless root canal speaks volumes about his skill and expertise. I highly recommend Dr. Grossman. His staff is wonderful too.
