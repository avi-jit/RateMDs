Biography

As a Physician, I chose the specialty of Child, Adolescent and Adult Psychiatry because of my passionate commitment to improve the quality of life of my patients. In order to provide the best care possible, I am committed, first and foremost, to being an attentive listener, vital to understanding patients' needs and goals. My approach is research-based, community standard and holistic, in that I integrate nutrition, mindfulness and non-pharmaceutical modalities wherever appropriate. No single method will be the best doctor-patient fit for everyone. I do read all reviews and incorporate the feedback into my practice. Due to HIPAA privacy laws, I am unable to respond individually to each review. In response to the exaggerated, inflammatory feedback, Psychiatrists in my field have observed a distinct pattern: Some former patients are disgruntled primary due to one of three reasons; 1. They are seeking narcotics, habit forming drugs for inappropriate conditions and situations, 2. They seek a medical disability based on a psychiatric condition which either does not exist or is not severe enough to warrant it, 3. I honor my contract and send a statement for balance due (deductible and/or no show fees) and the patient refuses to pay. In short, my practice is thriving for a very simple reason: My respect for -and honesty with- my patients at all times. I am gratified that referrals from past patients constitutes the vast bulk of my practice. I appreciate all feedback, and will continue to provide the best, individualized psychiatric care possible to my patients.
