Biography

Dr. Bhullar was born in the small town of Kitimat in northern B.C. After high school, he attended Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, BC to complete his Bachelor of Science degree in Kinesiology, focusing on body physiology, disease and nutrition. Dr. Bhullar then moved to Boston, Massachusetts in the United States to complete his Doctorate of Optometry at the New England College of Optometry.
