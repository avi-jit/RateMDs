Biography

We follow the basic TCM theory to investigate and diagnose individual health related issues which mainly focus on the physical, but integrate all aspects of the person. Our office treats Gastrointestinal, Cardiovascular, Endocrine related, Respiratory, Immune System, Dermatological, Genitourinary, Reproductive, Mental and Emotional issues, Aging and Recovery from illness and surgery. We offer the treatment of acute and chronic musculoskeletal and neurological pain through Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine.
