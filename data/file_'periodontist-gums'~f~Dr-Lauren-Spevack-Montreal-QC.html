Biography

Dr. Lauren Spevack received a Bachelor of Science degree in biology, and a Doctorate of Dental Medicine from McGill University. She continued her post-graduate training in Rochester, New York, at the Eastman Institute for Oral Health leading to a certification in the specialty of Periodontology. She later completed an Implantology Fellowship where she received advanced training in prosthetically-driven dental implant treatment planning and therapy in the Prosthodontic Department at the University of Rochester. As a fellow of the Royal College of Dentists of Canada, she is a certified specialist in periodontics and implantology. Furthermore, she is a certified Diplomate of the American Board of Periodontology. Dr. Spevack is a Faculty Lecturer and Clinical Instructor at McGill University in the Department of Dentistry, in the Multidisciplinary Residency Programs at the Montreal General and Montreal Children’s Hospitals. She is also an Assistant Clinical Professor in the Periodontology Department at the University of Rochester.......... Dre. Spevack a ensuite obtenu un Fellowship en Implantologie dans le Département Prothétique de l’Université Rochester, où elle a reçu un entrainement intensif dans les méthodes de traitement d’implantation prothétique dentaire. Elle est chargée de cours et instructeur clinique dans le Département de Médecine Dentaire de l’Université McGill et dans les programmes de résidence multidisciplinaire à l'Hôpital Général de Montréal et à l'Hôpital de Montréal pour Enfants. Elle est aussi Assistante Professeur Clinique dans le Département de Parodontologie de l’Université Rochester.
