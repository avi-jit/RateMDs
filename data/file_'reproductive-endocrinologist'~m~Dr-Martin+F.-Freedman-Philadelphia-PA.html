Biography

Dr. Freedman received his medical degree from Wayne State University and completed his postgraduate fellowship in Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility at the University of Pennsylvania Medical Center. He was responsible for the first cryopreserved embryo pregnancy in the Mid-Atlantic region. He is a co-founder and past president of the Philadelphia Area Reproductive Endocrine Society. He is board certified in OB/GYN and sub-specialty board certified in Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility. Dr. Freedman is a reviewer for the journal Fertility and Sterility.
