Biography

Ronald J. Iannacone, DO, FAOCO is a board certified adult and pediatric Otolaryngologist (surgeon of the ear, nose, throat, head and neck) and facial plastic surgeon. His clinical work includes medical and surgical diagnosis and treatment of the sinuses, specializing in minimally invasive endoscopic sinus surgery, snoring and sleep apnea, hearing and balance disorders, as well as cosmetic surgery and treatments such as Botox, Restylane, and chemical peels. Dr. Iannacone received his BS from Michigan State University and his medical degree from The University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey. He completed his residency in Michigan with training at Botsford and Oakland General Hospitals and The Children’s Hospital of Michigan. Dr. Iannacone is a Fellow of The American Osteopathic College of Otolaryngology-Head and Neck Surgery, a member of the New Jersey Association of Osteopathic Physicians and Surgeons, The New Jersey Medical Society, The American Academy of Osteopathy, and The American Medical Association.
