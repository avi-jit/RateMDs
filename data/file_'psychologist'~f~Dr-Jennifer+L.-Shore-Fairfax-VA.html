Biography

Dr. Shore is a licensed clinical psychologist with a wide range of experiences that enable her to listen, encourage, and support a variety of populations. Located in downtown Fairfax, Virginia, she conducts evaluations/assessments as well as counseling services for children, adolescents, and adults experiencing a variety of stressors. Dr. Shore uses an integrative approach to therapy which allows her to tailor overall therapeutic goals as well as target each individual session to meet your needs at that point in time.
