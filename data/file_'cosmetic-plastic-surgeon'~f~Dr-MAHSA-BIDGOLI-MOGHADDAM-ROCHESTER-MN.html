Biography

Dr. Mahsa Moghaddam Smoot is a Mayo Clinic trained plastic surgeon specializing in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery. Dr. Moghaddam Smoot received her Doctor of Medicine degree from the University of Sydney in Australia, and completed her residency training both in general surgery and plastic surgery at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN. Dr. Moghaddam Smoot joined Dr. Ralph Bashioum at the Bashioum Cosmetic Surgery Center, Ltd. in November 2013. Her soft-spoken, relaxed manner puts patients at ease, and her empathetic nature allows her to focus on the individual concerns and needs of each patient. Her practice covers all aspects of aesthetic and reconstructive surgery.
