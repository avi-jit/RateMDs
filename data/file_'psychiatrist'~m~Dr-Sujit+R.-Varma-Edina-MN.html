Biography

Dr. Varma is board certified by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology (ABPN) and has been working in the mental health field since 1997. In addition to psychiatric medication management, Dr. Varma offers the following services: •Saliva drug testing through Cordant labs – done on the premises •Personalized prescribing through Genesight by salivary DNA testing •Vivitrol monthly injections for alcohol and opioid dependence •He is certified by the DEA to prescribe suboxone, zubsolv, bunavail for opioid dependence •He performs Electro Convulsive Therapy at Fairview Southdale hospital •He works with a Ketamine infusion clinic for Treatment Resistant Depression (TRD)
