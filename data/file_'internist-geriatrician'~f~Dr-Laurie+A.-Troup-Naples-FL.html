Biography

Recently, there've been considerable changes in the doctor-patient relationship based upon Government mandates which increase documentation and reduce time spent in direct patient care. Both patients and physicians have voiced dissatisfaction with the current standard of medical care. Midwest Concierge of Naples will provide the personalized, quality care that patients desire and deserve by limiting the total practice size to less than 5% of a traditional internal medicine practice. With extended office visits, Dr. Troup will truly get to know each patient as an individual, not a list of diagnoses. Dr. Troup is not employed by a hospital or a large corporation. She is here to optimize your healthcare and well being, and will work to exceed your expectations.
