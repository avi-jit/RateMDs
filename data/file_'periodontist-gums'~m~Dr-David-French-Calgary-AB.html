Biography

David French B. Sc. , D.D.S., Dipl. Periodontics . Education: - University of Alberta BSc 1987 - University of Alberta graduating with the Gold Medal/ first in class in 1991 - Specialist Diploma Periodontics at the University of British Columbia 1994 - Calgary full-time private practice in Periodontics and Dental Implants 1994-2015 Community activity - President of the Calgary & District Dental Society in 2000. - Founder Comprehensive Treatment Planning Study Club 1994-2014 - Founder Alberta Dental Implant Academy - Straumann Surgical mentorship program Research and Teaching - Part time clinical instructor and researcher at University British Columbia - Fellow of the International Team of Implantology (ITI). - Member American Academy of Periodontology - Member American Academy of Osseointegration & European Academy of Osseointegration - published author o Multivariate survival analysis retrospective open cohort of 4591 implants (COIR ) o Performance of zirconia abutments 1-12 years (COIR) o Tri-factorial classification for osteotome mediated sinus elevation (Quintessence) o Osteotome mediated sinus lift success – survival ( in press IJPRD) o Papillon Lefevre long-term case report JCDA o Immediate Implant Loading case report JCDA o 8 year clinical and radiographic results -GBR case report (Quintessence) o 5 year follow up of mandibular short straumann implant ( GJOS) Speaker: - National conference: Canadian Academy of Periodontology 2008 and 2012. - Biocare global forum Ontario Canada June 2015 - International Team Implantology ( ITI ) National Meeting Sept 2015
