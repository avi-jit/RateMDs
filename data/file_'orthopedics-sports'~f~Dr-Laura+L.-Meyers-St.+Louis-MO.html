Biography

Dr. Meyers is a board-certified orthopedic surgeon who completed medical school, an intership and her residency at Vanderbilt University Medical Center in Nashville, Tenn. She completed her fellowship in Pediatric Orthopedic Surgery at Children’s Hospital and Health Center in San Diego. She traveled to South America and Mexico during her studies with Kids First Surgical Missions. Her hobbies include aviation and athletics.
