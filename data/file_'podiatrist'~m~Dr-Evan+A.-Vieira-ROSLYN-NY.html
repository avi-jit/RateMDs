Biography

Dr. Evan Vieira was the Chief Resident at NSUH-LIJ Medical Center before he joined the Associated Podiatrists practice in Long Island, NY. Dr. Vieira treats every patients as if he were treating a family member and is very knowledgeable and caring. He has great ties to the Long Island Community and lives in Roslyn with his wife and young daughter. He also speaks medical Spanish.
