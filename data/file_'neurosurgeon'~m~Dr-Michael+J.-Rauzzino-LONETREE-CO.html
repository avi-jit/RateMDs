I had an ACDF and partial corpectomy on C5-6 and C6-7 on March 20. Dr. Rauzzino and Steven were both extremely professional and gave detailed information on my condition. I told Dr. Rauzzino that I was very afraid of the swallowing problems that are associated with moving the esophagus out of the way for the surgery. I had two other neurosurgeon opinions where I was told I was going to need a two-level corpectomy. Dr. Rauzzino is the only one who said he could fix my problem without tearing out my vertebrae. Also, he went out of his way to minimize the moving of my esophagus, and I had very minimal swallowing problems. He is an excellent surgeon, and Steven is an excellent physician's assistant. Dr. Rauzzino even gave us his cell phone number in case of an emergency. All in all, the surgery was not as bad as I thought it would be. I cannot express my gratitude enough to Dr. Rauzzino and his staff.
