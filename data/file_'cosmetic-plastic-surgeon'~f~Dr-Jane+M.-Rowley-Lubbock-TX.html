Biography

Dr. Jane Rowley grew up in Lubbock, the daughter of a long time Lubbock plastic surgeon Dr. Milton Rowley. She received unparalleled plastic surgery training at the Institute for Reconstructive Plastic Surgery at New York University. Dr. Rowley has been practicing in Lubbock since 1997, longer than any surgeon with the exception of her father. At Rowley Plastic Surgery we specialize in cosmetic and reconstructive procedures and offer exceptional care with confidence and communication.
