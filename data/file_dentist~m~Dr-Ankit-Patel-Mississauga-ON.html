Biography

Also known as Dr. A, he was born and raised in Thornhill, Ontario. He attended Vaughan Secondary School, where he graduated with honors in 2002. He then went to Michigan to pursue his undergraduate degree in Biochemistry at Ferris State University, where he was named to the Dean’s list. Dr. A applied to dental school and was awarded early admission to the University of Detroit Mercy, and was among the youngest to graduate at the tender age of 24. Dr. A is licensed by the Royal College of Dental Surgeons of Ontario, and is a member of the Ontario Dental Association, Canadian Dental Association and Academy of General Dentistry. He has also completed a 1 year residency in Dental Implant training. Dr. A spends a lot of his free time with his family friends, at the gym or travelling. Lastly, Dr. A is single, loves writing poetry and taking long walks on the beach after eating a candlelit dinner he prepared from scratch.
