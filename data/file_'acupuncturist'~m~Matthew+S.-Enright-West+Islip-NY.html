Biography

Matthew Enright is a licensed Acupuncturist and currently practices acupuncture in Boca Raton, FL and New York, NY. Matthew is a wellness-driven acupuncturist, with 13 years’ experience serving diverse populations with an impressive background running highly profitable practices. In 1997, Matthew Enright graduated with a Bachelor's degree in medical illustration from R.I.T. He has collaborated with physicians from operating rooms, hospitals and clinics to develop detailed and extensive illustrations for patient education and has produced conceptual designs from both cadaver and live subjects. Matthew has served as Chief Medical Illustrator for “A Compendium of Degenerative Brain Diseases with Section on Neurophysiology and Neuropharmacology”, “Three Needle Techniques" and has also served as Author and Chief Medical Illustrator for “Anatomy of Acupuncture; An Illustrated Point Location Manual” published in 2015. Matthew has also been published as an Acupuncturist specifically pertaining to Integrative Oncology and sits on an expert panel with Expert Beacon advising cancer patients on available modalities and treatments available. In 2002, Matthew graduated top of his class with a Master's degree with a double major in acupuncture and Oriental Medicine from the Atlantic Institute of Oriental Medicine. Matthew is a licensed Acupuncturist in Florida and New York. As well as being a successful medical illustrator and a licensed Acupuncturist, he is also an adjunct professor at NOVA Southeastern University teaching Integrative medicine and the fundamentals of Traditional Chinese Medicine in the Bachelor of Health Sciences program. Matthew is also an adjunct professor at the Atlantic Institute of Oriental Medicine teaching Practice and Business Management and the Course Director and Chief Instructor of the “Basic Acupuncture Program” at the University of Miami, Miller School of Medicine and an acupuncture consultant for the University Of Miami, Miller School of Medicine. Matthew has also served as a business consultant for several financial and marketing firms dealing with client or department heads to define needs or problems, perform research, studies and surveys to obtain and analyze data to advise on or recommend solutions utilizing knowledge of theory, principles or technology of specific discipline or field of specialization.
