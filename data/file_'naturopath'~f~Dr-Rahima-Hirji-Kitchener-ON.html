Dr. Rahima Hirji's response
Thank you for your feedback. Unfortunately, people need to spend months and sometimes years before the treatments have a cumulative effect and start to make a prolonged difference. Often, we will say that it will take a month of treatments for every year you have been unwell. I may not have made the long-term commitment clear and for that, I apologize. I focus on diet. We eat everyday. What we consume will have an enormous effect on our overall health. In my practice, I believe nothing can be healed without working on diet and gut. I do not prescribe herbals because they are actually more costly than the combination capsules and powders we carry and the herbal tinctures often taste horrendous. In my experience, people will purchase herbals based on a recommendation and then the bottle will be left untouched due to the inconvenience and taste. Instead we sell combination herbal capsules, vitamins, minerals and other professional combinations that have better patient compliance. It is true that they can be expensive. We keep our markup to industry minimums but certainly, it would be nice if the supplements were OHIP covered. I hope that you have found a practitioner that has been able to help you and that you are feeling better. In health, Dr. Rahima, ND
