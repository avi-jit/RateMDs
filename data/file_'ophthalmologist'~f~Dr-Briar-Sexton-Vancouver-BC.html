I have been seeing Dr. Sexton for many years following a family eye condition. She may not be the most cheerful doctor (definitely not much small talk), but she knows her stuff and is very knowledgeable. She was more invested in getting to know her patients back in the day, but I don’t blame her for losing patience after so many years of practice. Regardless she is still a very knowledgeable doctor in her field and I trust her completely. Although her appointments take a long time (sometimes up to 2 hours), her staff does give me a heads up and tell me to bring headphones & music while I wait. Their booking system is now all electronic and make it very easy for patients to confirm appointments or rebook if necessary. However, I can see how this may be a challenge for patients who are not used to an online booking system. Overall I am satisfied with having her as my ophthalmologist.
