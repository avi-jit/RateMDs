Biography

Ever since he was young, Dr. Mark Gasparini always wanted to be in the medical field. To start his education and career path towards being a doctor, he studied at Fairfield University where he earned his B.S. in Biology. He then completed his Doctorate of Podiatric Medicine from the New York College of Podiatric Medicine and completed his podiatric surgery and medicine residencies through the Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center at Northport, New York. Currently, Dr. Gasparini is a member of the American Podiatric Medical Association, New York State's Podiatric Medical Association, and is involved in many other associations and boards. He is also vertified in other areas, including wound care and hyperbaric oxygen treatment.
