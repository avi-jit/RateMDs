Biography

Dr. Septon is a Board Certified Dermatologist practicing medical, cosmetic, and surgical Dermatology for patients of all ages. She actively manages all types of skin conditions and enjoys performing outpatient surgical procedures. Dr. Septon regularly performs cosmetic procedures for patients offering Botox®, Xeomin®, Belotero®, Radiesse®, Juvederm®, chemical peeling, laser, and sclerotherapy. Dr. Septon has dedicated her career to ethical, compassionate, patient-focused care. She possesses strong clinical skills with judgement to quickly and accurately assess a patient’s condition and develops a total care plan that addresses the patient’s priorities while meeting their healthcare needs. Dr. Septon regularly improves office operations and efficiencies and has incorporated electronic medical record (EMR) systems in her practices. She enjoys working within a team environment to ensure excellent patient care and a positive work atmosphere. Dr. Septon has advanced training in filler, neurotoxin, and laser techniques. She is respected by her colleagues and is often referred challenging cases by local Dermatologists because of her clinical expertise. Her commitment to excellence and likeability factor results in a high number of return patients and referrals. Dr. Septon is a native Chicagoan and completed her medical education and residency training there. She is a second-generation Dermatologist and practiced in Chicagoland before moving to New York. She enjoys spending time with her family including her two young children.
