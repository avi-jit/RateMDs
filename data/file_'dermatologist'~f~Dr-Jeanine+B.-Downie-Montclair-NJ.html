Biography

Montclair, New Jersey is home to Dr. Jeanine Downie, who is a dermatologist with extensive skill and qualifications in providing a range of dermatology services. Her practice, Image Dermatology, is a premier dermatology facility that serves the people of Montclair and the surrounding areas. Dr. Downie has equipped her practice with the latest technology and tools in the field of dermatology in order to offer her patients a consistently high standard of dermatology care.She is board-certified by the American Board of Dermatology. In addition to this, Dr. Downie has been honored by Castle Connolly as one of the Top Doctors of New York Metropolitan in 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, and 2012. After earning her Bachelor of Science degree in Biology and Psychology from the Tufts University, Dr. Downie attended the American University to earn her Master of Arts degree in Biology. She then went on to attend the SUNY-Health Science Center located in Brooklyn to earn her Doctor of Medicine degree. Dr. Downie followed this by completing a pediatric residency which lasted two years at the New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center. Later on, Dr. Downie completed her residency in dermatology at the Mount Sinai Medical Center located in New York City. At her practice in Montclair, Dr. Downie offers a range of dermatology services to her patients. Among these services are Botox injections, Sclerotherapy, Exilis body contouring and fat reduction treatment, Vanquish Fat Melting, Chemical Peels, Restylane, Fraxel Restore, Perlane, Laser Hair Removal, Juvederm, Patch Testing, Sculptra, Radiesse, and Skin Cancer Surgery.
