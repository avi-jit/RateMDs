Biography

Dr. Alexander Wong is an infectious diseases specialist in Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada. He has a large outpatient practice located in the Infectious Diseases Clinic at the Regina General Hospital. His clinical focuses include HIV and viral hepatitis including hepatitis B and hepatitis C, and all general infectious diseases. He works with a dedicated multidisciplinary team including nurses, pharmacists, social workers, and dieticians. He prides himself on providing high-quality, respectful, patient-centered care. He also is extensively involved in research activities including clinical trials and cohort studies in patients with HIV and viral hepatitis, and collaborates nationally and internationally on many such studies to help drive the research agenda locally in the province of Saskatchewan.
