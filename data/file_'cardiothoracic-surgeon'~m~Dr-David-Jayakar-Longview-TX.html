Biography

Dr. DJ and his team are proud of their history of innovative techniques, tradition of quality patient care and unparalleled experience in heart bypass and valve surgery. Whether you are a patient, family member or medical professional, we want you to benefit from their knowledge and work. They specialize on: Beating heart surgery or off-pump surgery (OPCABG), Minimally invasive heart surgery, Aortic valve surgery, Mitral valve repair, Pacemakers, Heart Failure surgery, Heart Reconstruction surgery, Vascular surgery, Thoracic surgery, Minimally invasive lung surgery and Esophageal surgery.
