Biography

Dr. Kurtin graduated from Michigan's Ferris State University with a Bachelor of Science in Applied Biology and work in Psychology. Four years later, he attained the Doctorate of Optometry degree (O.D.) at the prestigious University of Houston Optometry school in Houston, Texas. Post graduate training includes certification to treat eye diseases, including Glaucoma, contact lens fitting for corneal issues, Keratoconus and corneal reshaping therapy to improve vision (CRT Orthokeratology). This doctor is also certified to fit Chromagen eyeglasses that are FDA cleared for children with reading or Dyslexic issues.
