Biography

Our practice has been providing comprehensive oral and maxillofacial surgical care in the Mid-Hudson Valley region for over 16 years. All efforts are tailored to the needs-both medical and financial-of our patients. All procedures performed in our office follow strict adherence to the most current Center of Disease Control and OSHA guidelines. We take pride in the fact that our practice is not high volume, as that allows us to provide the highest quality of individualized care available in the Hudson Valley area. Our patients are not rushed or pressured , as we do not aspire to a "corporate production healthcare model" . Our practice currently treats generations of families throughout the Tri-State area. We emphasize respect for the patient and are strong advocates for practical case management solutions. Dr. Matthew Hilmi is one of the only oral and maxillofacial surgeons to actively provide hospital care in the Mid-Hudson Valley region. His skills where acquired through years of hospital residency training at a Level 1 Trauma Center and perfected with over 23 years of post-graduate practice and education. We at Mid-Hudson Oral and Maxillofacial, PC invite you to join our family of caring professionals. Our practice is located in the Benedictine Medical Arts Building in Kingston, NY and our doctor is a active member of the surgical staff at the local hospitals. Please feel free to ask our staff members about the treatment options that are offered in the practice. Referrals are appreciated and emergencies are welcomed.
