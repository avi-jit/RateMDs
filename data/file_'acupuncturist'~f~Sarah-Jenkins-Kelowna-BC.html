Biography

I am a registered acupuncturist and Practitioner of Traditional Chinese Medicine. I graduated from a 4 year Acupuncture & Chinese medicine program in Victoria, BC in 2005. I have a special interest in treating hormone imbalances, digestive problems, autoimmune disease, neurological disorders and pain - these bodily systems are often interconnected and respond well to treatment with acupuncture and Chinese medicine. In 2012, I was seeking deeper theoretical knowledge in Chinese Medicine to sharpen my clinical skill set, and I came across The Institute of Classics of East Asian Medicine in Portland, Oregon (www.iceam.org), Since graduating as a diplomate from that program in 2015, I continue to make regular trips to Portland to study with my mentor, pursuing fellowship status with ICEAM. This program has given me the ability to sharpen my diagnostic skill set and take my understanding of Chinese Medicine (in particular herbs) to a level that is capable of treating serious and complex health issues. I have also completed a year long postgraduate program with the Xing Lin Institute that explores the historical roots of acupuncture along with modern applications with Dr. Ed Neal (www.xinglininstitute.com) When not practising and studying Acupuncture and Chinese medicine, I can usually be found spending time with my family, usually outdoors. I love living in the Okanagan and try to get outside as much as possible to ski, run, hike and swim.
