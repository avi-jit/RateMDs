Dr. Daher is very friendly and takes the time to answer any questions and clearly explain/outline treatment procedures. His staff are also very knowledgable and kind. At the downtown location in Calgary, they even pay for your parking every time you have an appointment (very important to someone on a tight budget). I was nervous about being rushed into making a decision immediately, and my experience was the exact opposite. Dr. Daher was very patient (I waited a month between the consultation appointment and actually signing up for the Invisalign Treatment) and offered to provide literature regarding the instrumentation that will be used during my treatment. Dr. Daher also walked me through all of my treatment options and provided the pros and cons to each. Overall, I would recommend this orthodontist to others. Thank you to Dr. Daher and his staff for answering all of my questions and for your professionalism.
