Biography

Dr. Tina Koopersmith is the founder of the West Coast Women's Reproductive Center in Southern California. Dr. Koopersmith is a Board-Certified in both Obstetrics and Gynecology, as well as a Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility . Her clinical areas of expertise include fertility, recurrent loss, premature ovarian failure and polycystic ovaries. In addition to providing fertility services, she specializes in gynecologic disorders, especially hormonal disturbances in young girls with difficulty during puberty and reproductive age women with irregular cycles. West Coast Women's Reproductive Center offers the very best in fertility testing and treatment options available today. Combining cutting-edge fertility treatment such as IVF, IUI, egg donation, surrogacy, egg freezing, robotic myomectomy, etc. with our doctors’ expertise and personal touch in a safe, caring environment. As a patient, you will receive a personalized fertility treatment plan based on your personal situation. We are committed to providing excellent clinical services and high pregnancy rates. We believe in working with couples in a teamwork atmosphere that empowers each member of the team, as they work together toward fertility solutions. We educate all our patients about reproductive physiology, new technologies and thoroughly discuss all possible options. We are dedicated to making the sometimes intimidating fertility treatment process as personalized and comfortable as possible. Tina Koopersmith, M.D., gives an unequaled level of personal attention to each and every one of her patients. We understand your passion to be a mother. That's why we created a medical fertility center where a team of professionals can help their fellow women.
