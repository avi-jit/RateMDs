Biography

Since 1996, I have provided skilled, compassionate acupuncture and Oriental medical care to patients at the Edmonton General Hospital, Guthrie Acupuncture Clinic and now at Penticton Integrative HealthCare, treating chronic and acute back pain, weight issues, glaucoma, menopausal symptoms, bed wetting, infertility, stress reduction, addiction cessation, fibromyalgia, chronic fatigue, reducing side effects of chemotherapy, myasthenia gravis, allergies, post-surgical pain, as well as conditions in the World Health Organization statement on acupuncture. I completed my medical training at the Oregon College of Oriental Medicine in Portland, www.ocom.edu , where I earned my graduate degree, Master of Acupuncture & Oriental Medicine (MAc&OM). I completed additional training in facial rejuvenation and in advanced acupuncture theory and methods, have studied and taught Kundalini yoga, and practice and teach Sheng Zhen chi gong www.shengzhen.org .
