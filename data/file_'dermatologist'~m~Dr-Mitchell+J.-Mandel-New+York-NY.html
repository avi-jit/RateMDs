Biography

Dr Mitchell J. Mandel is a board certified Dermatologist practicing over 20 years in Manhattan. Dr. Mandel graduated from New York University School of Medicine in 1989 and completed his internship in internal medicine in 1990. He completed his residency in Dermatology and Dermatological Surgery at the University of Miami School of Medicine/Jackson Memorial Hospital. Dr. Mandel has co-authored various scientific papers, as well as conducted numerous clinical trials. Dr. Mandel’s practice is unique in that it combines Medical Dermatology with Laser, Surgical and Cosmetic Dermatology in an outpatient setting. With access to cutting – edge research and ingredients coupled with his knowledge about skincare, Dr. Mandel formulated his own specialty line of skincare and anti – aging products called Skin Doctor ®. With patient feedback at the cornerstone of product development, Skin Doctor ® taps the most synergistic blends of clinically-proven ingredients and sophisticated technologies to address a variety of skincare challenges caused by environmental assaults and just plain genetics. The brand delivers visibly remarkable results and rave reviews from patients. Dr. Mandel is a volunteer attending physician at Mount Sinai Hospital in New York, a member of the New York County Medical Society, the Medical Society of the State of New York, and is a Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology.
