Dr. James Bonaparte's response
Thanks for your review. We are sorry your experience was not what you hoped for. Dr Bonaparte will never consider surgery for someone if he thinks it is either Dangerous or will not be effective. Biography

Dr James P. Bonaparte, MD, MSc, FRCSC is a Facial Cosmetic Surgeon and a Head and Neck Surgeon who has received a number of awards and recognitions for both his surgical skills as well as his academic achievements. He is a Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada, a Fellow of the Canadian Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery and has completed a prestigious fellowship with the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery.
