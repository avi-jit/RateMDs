Biography

Dr. Andre Berger has decades of experience in the medical field and has focused his practice in the areas of anti-aging and cosmetic rejuvenation since 2003. Widely considered to be a pioneer in the integration of cosmetic procedures and anti-aging medicine, he will work with you develop a customized treatment plan that builds a foundation for good daily health and promotes overall beauty. To find out how Rejuvalife Vitality Institute can help you live a long, healthy and beautiful life, please call (310) 975-3900 today to schedule a cosmetic consultation with Dr. Berger.
