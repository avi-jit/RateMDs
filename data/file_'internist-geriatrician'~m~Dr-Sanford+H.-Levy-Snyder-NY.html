Biography

Dr. Levy is an integrative/holistic medicine specialist, board certified in both Internal Medicine (1989) and Integrative Holistic Medicine (2003). Services offered to you include wellness care, healthy aging, guidance regarding dietary supplements and prevention of chronic disease, and treatment of a wide variety of chronic conditions, using a functional medicine approach. My personal goal is to listen to you and hear what your needs are, collaborate with you and to help you to optimize your health.
