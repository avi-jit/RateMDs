Biography

Orthopedic surgeon, specialized in shoulder, elbow and arthroscopy. He has completed a 2 years post-graduate fellowship in upper extremity reconstruction, sport medicine and arthroscopy. He was part of the medical team responsible for the Toronto Raptors (NBA) in 1995, and he is an international consultant for the Cirque du Soleil. Dr Beauchamp has published many articles in peer reviewed orthopedic surgery literature, and is involved in clinical reaserch and technology development.Since 1996, dr Beauchamp has proceeded to more than3500 arthroscopic reconstructions (one of the widest experience in Canada). Opted out of the public system in 2005 (i.e. Private practice), He can now see new patients with virtually zero waiting time at his own clinic, ExceptionMD, 10 min. away from Montreal international airport. Trained in ultasound imaging, Dr Beauchamp can provide clinical and radiological assessment during the first visit. Most of his surgical procedures are performed as outpatient (same day), and under local or regional anesthesia. Attentive follow-up and optimal accessibility are key elements of his medical philosophy.
