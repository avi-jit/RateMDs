Biography

Dr. Greenwald is a fellowship trained Neurosurgeon specializing in complex and minimally invasive surgery of the brain and spine. His practice encompasses the entire field of neurosurgery. His special surgical skills include minimally invasive spinal procedures as well as spinal instrumentation techniques. Part of his practice includes head and spine trauma.
