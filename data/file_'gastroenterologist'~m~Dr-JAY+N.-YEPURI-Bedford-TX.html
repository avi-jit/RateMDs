Biography

Jay Yepuri, MD is a board certified gastroenterologist in Bedford, TX. Dr. Yepuri sees patients for all digestive conditions including colon cancer screening and prevention by colonoscopy. Common conditions Dr. Yepuri sees patients in the Fort Worth area for include (but are not limited to) stomach pains, heartburn, reflux, rectal bleeding, constipation, diarrhea and swallowing difficulties. He finds that patient satisfaction is highest when patients are able to effectively participate as a partner in their healthcare decisions. If you are looking for expert digestive health care in the Fort Worth area, please call the practice for an appointment.At its core, the practice of medicine is based on the trust between a patient and their physician. Dr. Yepuri does his best to communicate with his patients as clearly and comprehensively as possible. He discusses all available options and works together as a team with his patients to develop a plan for treatment.
