Biography

Dr. Alibhai graduated from McGill Medical School in Montreal, Canada. He is the medical director of md cosmetic & laser clinic in Abbotsford and Port Moody, BC. He is a Past-President of the Canadian Association of Aesthetic Medicine. He is a clinical instructor for the UBC Faculty of Medicine. He has written articles in several aesthetic magazines and journals and frequently appears in the media as an expert in aesthetic medicine. Dr. Alibhai has been training physicians and dentists on various applications in aesthetic medicine since 2002 and has trained over 1,000 doctors worldwide. He is an internationally recognized speaker and author in the field of aesthetic medicine.
