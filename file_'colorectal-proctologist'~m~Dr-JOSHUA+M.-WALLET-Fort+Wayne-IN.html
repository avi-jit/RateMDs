Biography

Dr. Joshua Wallet is a colon & rectal surgeon in Fort Wayne, Indiana and is affiliated with multiple hospitals in the area, including Lutheran Hospital, Dupont Hospital, St. Joseph's Hospital, and Parkview Hospital. He received his medical degree from Jefferson Medical College and surgical training at York Hospital in Pennsylvania. He completed his Colon and Rectal Surgery specialty training at Thomas Jefferson University Hospital in Philadelphia.
