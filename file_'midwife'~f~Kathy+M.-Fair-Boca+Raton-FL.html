When I found out I was pregnant, I was still undecided whether I wanted to see a midwife. So I told my husband that we'll go see the midwife and if we don't feel comfortable we'll stick with my doctor. Well..I searched for midwives in the Boca area and stumbled upon great ratings for Kathy Fair. And I was glad to have found her! From the very first visit she sat down and took the time to answer all of our questions and even told us what to expect in each stage of my pregnancy. One time during my appointment she got a call that one of her patients was in labor and she had to go the hospital. When it came time for me to give birth, unfortunately it was the same day Kathy had to have knee surgery so Christine came for my labor...and she was great too! She was so patient and honored my birth plan. 3 days later Kathy even came to visit me and her other patients who were in the hospital even though she was limping around with a knee brace. From now on I'm going to this office for my annual checkups. On another note, which doctors do you know would take time out of their schedule to teach their own birth class? That showed me they really care about their patients.
