Biography

Welcome to Montreal West Island Orthodontist! Our team is dedicated in providing quality specialized care and individualized personal attention. Implementing the expertise of a skilled and experienced professional, coupled the latest technological advances, to keep your treatment to the absolute minimum. Our new and modern facilities include up-to-date sterilization procedures to maintain the highest levels of safety for our patients. We are equipped with the state of the art digital imaging technology and computer assisted analysis for ultimate precision. We specialize in offering Invisalign, SPEED and Damon braces. Dr. Chamma first obtained his School of Dental Surgery D.D.S. degree. He subsequently received his Orthodontic specialty training from the faculty of graduate studies, Orthodontic Department, where he obtained his Master of Science degree from the renowned University of Manitoba, one of the few universities in Canada to offer this full 3 year M.Sc. degree program as opposed to a certificate. Dr. Chamma returned to Montreal, after spending some time at the University of Toronto and the University of Manitoba, while he was preparing for the national Dental examination Board of Canada. Shortly thereafter, the Quebec Board of Dental Examiners recognized Dr. Chamma as a licensed Orthodontic Specialist. Dr. Chamma routinely attends continuing education seminars in the field of Orthodontics, throughout Canada and United States.
