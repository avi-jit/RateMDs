Biography

Mordecai N Klein, MD, FACC began practicing cardiology in 1991, and since 2005, has been practicing at The Medical Center of Plano. Dr. Klein is the director of Interventional Cardiology at the hospital and his practice specializes in diagnosing and treating all heart related issues. Also, Dr. Klein is a member of The American College of Phlebology and is an expert in the treatment of varicose veins.
